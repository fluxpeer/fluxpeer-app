## How to accept

Sign off each commit with the [Developer Certificate of Origin](https://developercertificate.org/)
(`git commit -s`) **and** indicate your agreement to this CLA in your first pull
request (e.g. via the project's CLA-assistant check, or by stating "I have read
and agree to the CLA" in the PR). A corporate/entity CLA is available on request
for contributions made on behalf of an organization.

> This is a practical, precedent-based template (modeled on the Apache ICLA),
> **not** formal legal advice. Have it reviewed by an open-source/IP attorney
> before relying on it commercially.
